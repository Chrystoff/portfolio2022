My Interests & Hobbies

Coding

Coding has always interested me! I've read many blogs and articles explaining the joy of seeming like a 'wizard' to friends and family. I've dabbled in it with small passion projects or small code courses. But doing a proper course now I know I enjoy it as much as I enjoyed the idea of it.

Golf

I've only recently started playing golf, my brother randomly said he was going to start playing and I just said i'd give it a go. I'm still new and i'm not any good but it seems the enjoyment of golf is the learning a skill rather than being good at a skill.

Tabletop Role-playing Games

A hobby I never expected to be so into and I only got into during the pandemic. I've suddenly found myself swamped in TTRPG books with the aim of get every high praised campaign and running them. Turns out there are a fair few critically acclaimed campaigns but I'm keen to get them going.

Movies

Movies have always been in my life. I wish I had more time for them now, but I try to keep my taste sharp but my brows mid-tier as I find some critically acclaimed movies to be a bit of a wank on contemporary culture and themes. I find myself watching older movies nowadays that I watched growing up that deliver a lot of nostalgia.

Warhammer 40k

Something I have always seen as 'cool'; 40k is a great hobby which teaches patience and also some arts and crafts skills generally how to blend and painting tricks I never knew i'd ever know about. I have yet to get into any battles but in time i'm sure my day will come. very satisfying hobby as the builds can be hard or tedious, but seeing the finished product is a beautiful thing!
